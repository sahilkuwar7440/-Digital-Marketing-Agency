## About Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is the practice of promoting businesses through online channels such as search engines, social media platforms, paid advertising, email marketing, and content marketing. A well-planned digital strategy helps organizations reach the right audience, increase brand awareness, and generate measurable business results.

Businesses of all sizes rely on digital marketing to strengthen customer relationships and remain competitive in today's digital marketplace.
